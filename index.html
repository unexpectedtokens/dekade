About us

Coffeeshop De Kade has been around for over 30 years. The shop has a very homely environment and is based in the south of Amsterdam, only a two minute walk from the Olympic Stadium. See route

Due to the constant quality of the products and the relaxed atmosphere, this shop attracts a wide variety of guests. The comprehensive menu has a solid base for the every day smoker but also a variety of different temporary exclusive strains.

De Kade is also known for the friendly staff that is always there to help you pick the product that suits you best! So after a nice day take your time to retreat from the chaos of everyday life. Take a moment to relax and enjoy our hidden gem. And don't forget to challenge someone for some table soccer!
